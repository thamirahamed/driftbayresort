Welcome to

Driftbay Resort

Luxury Beach Resorts in Sri Lanka

Nestled along the pristine coastline, Driftbay Resort welcomes you to a haven where time stands still and the rhythms of the ocean set the pace. Our commitment to uncompromising luxury is reflected in every detail, from the thoughtfully designed accommodations to the world-class hospitality that greets you. Embark on culinary journeys that celebrate the finest local ingredients and international flavors.

Whether you choose to bask in the sun's warm embrace, explore the vibrant culture of Sri Lanka, or simply revel in the comforts of your private oasis, Driftbay Resort promises an experience that transcends imagination. Welcome to a retreat where luxury meets nature's beauty in perfect harmony.

Villas & Suites

Discover refined luxury in our Driftbay Resort suites. With modern elegance and coastal charm, each suite offers spacious living, plush furnishings, and stunning ocean views. Unwind on your private balcony or relax in the cozy king-sized bed, while attentive service ensures an extraordinary stay.

Experience unrivaled opulence at Driftbay Resort's villas. These private retreats feature pools, sleek furnishings, and breathtaking ocean panoramas. Enjoy chef-prepared meals, spa-like bathrooms, and exclusive living, all amidst the beauty of the coast.

Dining

Experience a culinary voyage from Asia to Italy at our resort. Discover the freshest seafood in diverse cuisines that celebrate vibrant colors and spices at our Driftbay restaurants:

  • Spice Isle - Sri Lankan Cuisine
  • Piazza - Italian Cuisine
  • Hanamichi - Japanese Cuisine

The hotel enforces a rigorous policy concerning cleanliness, food hygiene, and freshness, all while creating an ideal dining atmosphere.

Our Spaces

Discover a world of luxury at Driftbay Resort, where our well-appointed suites and villas provide stunning views and unparalleled comfort. We've got:

  1.   Spa
  2.   Fitness Centre
  3.   Swimming Pools
  4.   Beach Cabanas

Rejuvenate your senses at our spa for holistic wellness, and stay active at the modern fitness center. As the day winds down, unwind in style at our beach cabanas. Driftbay Resort promises unforgettable moments amidst these exceptional facilities
